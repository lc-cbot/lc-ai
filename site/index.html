SOC Teams

Each team is a coordinated pipeline of specialized AI agents. Scroll through to see the architecture of each.

Tiered SOC

Full-featured Agentic SOC modeled after the traditional L1/L2/L3 structure. Eight specialized agents form a coordinated pipeline that handles the complete alert lifecycle.

$0.10 - $15.00 /alert Best for: stable orgs wanting defense-in-depth
4 Agents

Lean SOC

Minimal Agentic SOC built for maximum autonomy with minimum complexity. Four agents handle the complete alert lifecycle without specialized tiers.

$0.10 - $8.00 /alert Best for: smaller orgs or getting started fast
7 Agents

Baselining SOC

Designed for newly onboarded organizations with high false positive volume. Processes detections in hourly batches and aggressively creates FP rules to reduce noise.

~$5.00 /hr bulk Best for: new orgs with noisy detections
3 Agents

Intel Team

Three-agent pipeline that runs daily to collect open-source threat intelligence, analyze it against your org's platform profile, and produce detection rules and IOC lookups.

~$12.00 /day max Best for: automated daily threat intel pipeline
3 Agents

Exposure Monitor

AI-powered SOC that runs daily to discover, scan, and report on your organization's external attack surface using free OSINT sources. No API keys beyond LC and Anthropic.

~$4.50 /day max Best for: automated external attack surface management
2 Agents

Breach & Attack Simulation

Daily automated breach simulation using Atomic Red Team (MITRE ATT&CK). Executes realistic multi-step attack scenarios, evaluates detection coverage, and engineers D&R rules for gaps.

~$18.00 /day max Best for: continuous detection validation and MDR coverage
4 Agents

MDR Hunting Pipeline

MSSP-oriented threat intelligence, detection engineering, and proactive threat hunting pipeline. Runs daily in a central org and operates across all tenant organizations.

~$32.00 /day max Best for: MSSPs wanting automated cross-tenant threat hunting
Mix & Match

Standalone Agents
